# Tawala POS

> Tawala biashara yako.

Tawala POS is a modern business management platform built for Kenyan businesses.

We help SMEs move from manual operations, notebooks, and guesswork to organized, automated, and intelligent business management.

Tawala is more than a POS system.

It is your digital business partner.

# Our Mission

To empower Kenyan businesses with simple, powerful, and affordable technology that helps them grow with confidence.
